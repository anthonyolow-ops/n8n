## Role Descriptions & Hiring Requirements

### 1. Project Manager (1 FTE)

**Reporting to:** Project Director

**Responsibilities:**
- Overall project coordination
- Timeline and budget management
- Stakeholder communication
- Risk management
- Team scheduling
- Status reporting

**Required Skills:**
- PMP or Agile certification
- 5+ years PM experience
- Experience with technical projects
- Stakeholder management
- Budget oversight
- Risk assessment

**Salary Range:** $100K - $140K

```

├─ Week 2-3: n8n Architect + Senior Backend
└─ Week 3-4: Business Analyst + Initial team planning

Month 2 (Weeks 5-8)
├─ Week 5-6: Mid/Junior Backend + DevOps Engineers
```
