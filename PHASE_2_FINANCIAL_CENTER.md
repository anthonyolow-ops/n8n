Phase 2 focuses on implementing the **Financial Center Operations** module - the core transaction processing, compliance, and settlement engine. This is the foundation upon which banking, satellite, and consultancy systems will operate.

**Phase 2 Goals:**
- ✅ Transaction processing pipeline fully operational
- ✅ Compliance & KYC/AML automation active
- ✅ Real-time settlement system
- ✅ Financial reporting dashboards
- ✅ Audit logging and compliance trails

- Handle 1000+ transactions/day
- 99.9% uptime for critical workflows
- <5 second transaction processing time
- Zero compliance violations
- Complete audit trail for all transactions

- Phase 1 must be complete
